Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for regular events and 2-3 months for weddings to ensure availability.

Do you offer food tasting sessions?

Yes! We offer complimentary tasting sessions for wedding bookings and paid tasting for other events.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7+ days before the event receive a full refund. Within 7 days, 50% of the advance is refundable.

Do you provide serving staff?

Yes, all our packages include professional serving staff. Additional staff can be arranged if needed.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We can customize menus for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary requirements.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Chennai and surrounding areas within 50km. Long-distance catering can be discussed.
